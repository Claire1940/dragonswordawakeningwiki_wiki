## Gameplay Mechanics

### Combat System

One of the standout features of DragonSword Awakening is its combat system. The game utilizes a status element system that adds depth to the gameplay. Enemies can be affected by various conditions, and your team members can chain additional attacks and abilities together to create a constant loop of triggering effects.

**Table 1: Status Elements**

| Element | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Fire    | Deals fire damage and has a chance to burn enemies |
| Water   | Cools down enemies and reduces their attack power |
| Earth   | Slows down enemies and makes them vulnerable to physical attacks |
| Wind    | Increases movement speed and evasiveness |

Understanding and utilizing these elements is crucial for successful combat. Make sure to rotate your team members effectively to capitalize on the synergies between their abilities.

## Tips & Tricks

### Character Skill Management

Focus on upgrading skills that complement your playstyle and the abilities of your party members. For example, if you have a lot of fire-based characters, investing in fire-related skills can be beneficial.

### Dungeon Strategy

Dungeons offer a great way to gain experience and materials. Prioritize the dungeons that offer the most rewards based on your current needs and character setup.

### Boss Fights

Boss fights can be challenging but also highly rewarding. Pay attention to the boss's attack patterns and use your team's abilities to exploit their weaknesses.
