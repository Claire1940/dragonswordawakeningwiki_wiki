## Character Progression and Unlocking Characters

One of the core aspects of DragonSword: Awakening is character progression and unlocking. As you progress through the story, you will unlock new characters with unique abilities and playstyles. Here's a breakdown of the process:

### Story Progression
To unlock characters, you must complete each chapter of the story. As you clear each chapter, you will receive tickets that can be exchanged for characters in the exchange shop. Additionally, completing the hero quest line will reward you with exclusive karma for specific heroes.

### Hero Quest Line
The hero quest line features exclusive quests for specific heroes. These quests are not available initially but unlock as you progress through the story. Completing these quests will reward you with exclusive karma for the character and their unique karma points.

### Karma and Dismantling
Karma is a currency that can be used to unlock and upgrade characters. If you have a character you no longer want to use, you can dismantle their karma to receive their currency and exclusive karma point, which can be exchanged for a different character in the exchange shop.

## Underwater Exploration and Mounts

DragonSword: Awakening offers a unique underwater exploration experience. To access underwater areas, you will need to unlock the right familiar, which can be done through mercenary leveling and story progression. One of the best mounts in the game is tied to story progression and can be unlocked at the very end of the game.

### Mount System
The mount system is very cool because each mount has its own unique skills. Unlocking another variation of the same mount will unlock the second skill for that mount, which can be used on all other mounts except a few unique ones.

## Combat System

The combat system in DragonSword: Awakening is complex yet rewarding. Each character has unique abilities and playstyles, and mastering the combat system will make the game even more enjoyable.

### Basic Combat Mechanics
- Basic attack
- Jump basic attack
- Active skills (signal, grab, etc.)
- Special abilities (air dash, dodge attacks, etc.)

### Cooldowns and Skill Management
Your skills have cooldowns, and the only way to reduce these cooldowns is by hitting enemies. When you reach a specific status effect or criteria, you can call in your teammates for support.

## Gear and Crafting

Each character has their own unique gear, and crafting is an essential part of character progression. To craft gear, you will need to gather materials and select the main stat for each gear slot.

### Gear Crafting
- Go to the blacksmith and select the gear you want to craft.
- Choose the main stat for each gear slot, ensuring the strongest stat is on the chest piece.
- Craft the gear using the gathered materials.

### Materials and Farming
Materials can be obtained by completing missions, exploring the world, and participating in activities. Farming materials can be time-consuming, so it's important to manage your time efficiently.
