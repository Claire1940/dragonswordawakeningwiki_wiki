## Character Unlocking and Progression

In DragonSword: Awakening, unlocking characters and progressing through the game is a key aspect of the experience. Here's how you can get started:

### Unlocking Characters

- **Story Progression**: Clear each chapter of the story to unlock tickets, which can be used to unlock new characters.
- **Hero Quest Line**: Complete exclusive quests for specific heroes to unlock exclusive karma and skills.
- **Mounts**: Unlock mounts through mercenary leveling and story progression. Some mounts are tied to story progression and can only be obtained at the end of the game.

### Character Progression

- **Leveling**: Characters level up by completing quests, battles, and exploring the world.
- **Skills**: Unlock new skills as your characters level up, including passive abilities and special moves.
- **Awakening**: Unlock your character's master skill by collecting grimoires found in hidden locations throughout the game.

## Combat Strategies

Combat in DragonSword: Awakening is a complex system that requires mastery to excel. Here are some key strategies to help you dominate in battle:

### Basic Combat Mechanics

- **Basic Attack**: Every character has a basic attack, which can be executed by pressing the attack button.
- **Jump Attack**: Every character can perform a jump attack by pressing the jump button while in the air.
- **Dodge**: Characters can dodge enemy attacks by pressing the dodge button.

### Advanced Combat Mechanics

- **Skills**: Characters have a limited number of skills that can be used to deal damage or perform special moves.
- **Signal Skill**: When an enemy is broken, you can call in your teammates to support your combo.
- **Grab Skill**: Characters can grab enemies to perform special moves or execute them.
- **Cooldowns**: Skills have cooldowns, and hitting enemies can reduce these cooldowns.
