## Introduction

Welcome to the ultimate beginner guide for DragonSword: Awakening! If you're new to this fantastic game, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to get started, from character unlocking to mastering combat and building your ultimate team. So, let's dive in!

## Unlocking Characters

In DragonSword: Awakening, you can unlock a variety of characters, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. To unlock characters, you need to clear the story mode and complete the hero quest lines. Here's a quick guide to help you get started:

- **Story Mode**: Clear each chapter of the story to unlock tickets, which you can use to unlock characters from the exchange shop.
- **Hero Quest Lines**: Complete exclusive quests for specific heroes to unlock their unique karma and abilities.

### Characters to Unlock

Here are some characters you should consider unlocking:

- **Theresius and Ornetes**: These heroes have cool cinematics and exclusive karma, making them great choices for beginners.
- **Charlotte**: A versatile ninja with a range of abilities, including shadow clones and daggers.
- **Raina**: An agile character with multiple dodge skills and a sprint ability.
- **Johnny**: A fast and powerful character with a unique helmaker skill.

## Mastering Combat

Combat in DragonSword: Awakening is complex but rewarding. Here's what you need to know to master it:

- **Basic Attacks**: Each character has basic attacks and jump attacks.
- **Skills**: Characters have active skills that can be used in combat. These skills have cooldowns, so you'll need to hit enemies to reduce them.
- **Dodge Attacks**: Some characters have dodge skills that allow them to avoid attacks and execute follow-up attacks.
- **Combos**: Combine basic attacks, jump attacks, and dodge attacks to create powerful combo moves.

### Tips for Combat

- **Learn Each Character's Abilities**: Understand the abilities of each character you unlock and how to use them effectively.
- **Focus on Enemy Patterns**: Pay attention to enemy patterns and use your skills to exploit them.
- **Use Cooldowns Wisely**: Time your skills to ensure you have them ready when you need them.

## Mount System

The mount system in DragonSword: Awakening allows you to explore the world and access new areas. Here's how to unlock and use mounts:

- **Story Progression**: Unlock mounts by progressing through the story mode.
- **Mercenary Leveling**: Some mounts can be unlocked by leveling up your mercenary.
- **Unique Mounts**: The deluxe edition of the game includes a baby dragon that allows you to air dash in any direction while gliding.

### Tips for Mounts

- **Unlock All Mounts**: Try to unlock all available mounts to access new areas and explore the world.
- **Use Mounts for Exploration**: Mounts can help you reach new areas and access hidden resources.

## Character Builds and Skill Synergies

Creating a strong character build and understanding skill synergies is crucial for success in DragonSword: Awakening. Here's how to get started:

- **Choose Your Main Stat**: For each gear slot, choose the main stat that benefits your character the most. For example, Charlotte benefits from critical damage and critical chance.
- **Stack Sub Stats**: Focus on stacking sub stats that complement your main stat, such as attack power and defense ignore.
- **Unlock Skills**: Unlock skills for your characters to increase their effectiveness in combat.
- **Understand Skill Synergies**: Some skills work well together, creating powerful combinations.

### Tips for Character Builds

- **Experiment with Builds**: Try different builds to find the one that works best for you.
- **Focus on Your Playstyle**: Choose builds that complement your playstyle, whether you prefer action or more relaxed playstyles.
