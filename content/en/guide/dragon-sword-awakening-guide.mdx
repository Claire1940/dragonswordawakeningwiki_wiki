## 📚 Introduction

DragonSword: Awakening is a game that offers a rich and immersive experience. The story progression is crucial for unlocking characters and understanding the game's mechanics. In this guide, we will cover everything from story progression to character builds, combat tips, and more.

### 🎯 Story Progression and Character Unlocking

To unlock characters in DragonSword: Awakening, you need to clear each chapter of the story. As you progress, you will receive tickets that can be used to unlock characters in the exchange shop. Additionally, completing the hero quest line will reward you with exclusive karma for specific characters.

### 🚀 Character Builds

Choosing the right character builds is essential for success in DragonSword: Awakening. We will discuss the best characters to unlock, their strengths, and how to build them for optimal performance.

### 🏋️ Combat Tips

The combat system in DragonSword: Awakening is complex but rewarding once mastered. We will provide tips on basic attacks, jump attacks, air and ground combos, and how to effectively use your skills and abilities.

## 🛡️ Gear and Crafting

Gear and crafting play a significant role in your character's strength and playstyle. We will guide you through the process of crafting gear, selecting the right stats, and farming materials efficiently.

### 🛠️ Crafting Gear

To craft gear, you need to visit the blacksmith and select the specific gear set for your character. You can choose a main stat that will be the strongest for that gear slot. Make sure to highlight the gear you want to craft and select the main stat by pressing the appropriate button.

### 🌿 Farming Materials

Farming materials is an essential part of the game. You can farm materials by completing missions, exploring the world, and participating in various activities. Make sure to prioritize farming materials for your main character and any other characters you plan to use.

## 🌊 Underwater Exploration

Underwater exploration is a rewarding aspect of DragonSword: Awakening. To explore underwater, you will need to have the right familiar with you. These mounts can be unlocked through mercenary leveling and story progression.

### 🐉 Unlocking Mounts

Mounts can be unlocked through mercenary leveling and story progression. Some of the best mounts are tied to story progression and can only be obtained at the very end of the game. However, the mounts you unlock up to chapter six should be sufficient for exploring all the game's underwater content.

## 🏆 Mastery Skills and Awakening Points

Mastery skills are powerful abilities that can be unlocked once your character reaches level 30. Awakening points can be used to awaken your character's skills and unlock new move sets.

### 🎯 Unlocking Mastery Skills

To unlock mastery skills, you need to find special maps hidden throughout the game. These maps are usually found after completing puzzles or exploring hidden areas. Once you find a map, you can use it to unlock your character's mastery skill.

### 🌟 Awakening Points

Awakening points are earned by completing certain activities and defeating enemies. These points can be used to awaken your character's skills and unlock new move sets.

## 🌈 Fragment of Sacred Light

The Fragment of Sacred Light is an important currency in DragonSword: Awakening. It can be obtained by opening chests, completing certain activities, and defeating mini bosses. This currency is used to upgrade your skills and enhance your gear.

### 🎯 Acquiring Fragment of Sacred Light

To acquire Fragment of Sacred Light, you can complete activities such as defeating mini bosses, participating in special challenges, and completing certain quests. These activities will reward you with the currency, which can be used to upgrade your skills and enhance your gear.

## 🛡️ World Bosses

World bosses are powerful enemies that can be found in various locations throughout the game. Defeating these bosses will reward you with basic karma for characters and other valuable items.

### 🎯 Finding and Defeating World Bosses

To find world bosses, you can navigate to the Mercenary section and select the World Boss tab. Choose the world boss you want to defeat and prepare your team accordingly. Defeating these bosses will reward you with valuable items and karma.
