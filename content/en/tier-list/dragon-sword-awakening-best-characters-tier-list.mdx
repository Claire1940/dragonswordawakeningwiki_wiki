As the main protagonist, Lute is the core combat character with flexible abilities and strong synergy with different team compositions. Her versatile skills make her a valuable asset in various combat scenarios.

### S Tier - Dragon Affinity Heroes

These top-tier heroes possess powerful abilities and strong damage potential, excelling in difficult encounters and major battles. They are especially effective against challenging enemies and boss fights.

## A Tier - Warrior Companions

Warrior Companions are reliable frontline fighters with high durability and consistent performance in exploration and boss battles. They excel in absorbing damage and protecting allies.

Spellcaster Heroes are strong ranged damage dealers that provide elemental attacks and useful status effect applications. They are ideal for controlling the battlefield and applying strategic advantages.

## B Tier - Support Heroes

Support Heroes are balanced characters that improve team survival through healing, buffs, and defensive utility. They play a crucial role in maintaining the team's health and support during challenging encounters.

## C Tier - Beginner-Friendly Heroes

Beginner-Friendly Heroes are accessible characters with simple mechanics that are useful during early progression and learning combat systems. They are a great starting point for new players.
