## 🌟 Character Progression and Unlock Methods

### 🎯 Unlocking Characters

In DragonSword: Awakening, characters can be unlocked through various methods, including story progression and completing hero quest lines. As you progress through the story, you will be rewarded with tickets that can be used to unlock characters from the exchange shop. Additionally, completing hero quest lines for specific heroes will reward you with exclusive karma, which can be exchanged for exclusive characters.

### 🏋️‍♂️ Building a Synergistic Team

One of the most important aspects of playing DragonSword: Awakening is building a team with synergistic characters. Characters with complementary abilities can work together to create powerful combinations and tackle the game's challenges more effectively. It's essential to experiment with different team compositions to find the one that suits your playstyle.

## 🎯 Story Progression and Mounts

### 📜 Story Progression

The story in DragonSword: Awakening is not just for narrative purposes; it also unlocks important progression systems in the game. As you progress through the story, you will unlock new characters, mounts, and abilities. We highly recommend exploring the story to fully experience the game's world and unlock all its secrets.

### 🚀 Unlocking Mounts

Mounts in DragonSword: Awakening can be unlocked through mercenary leveling and story progression. These mounts provide unique skills and can significantly enhance your exploration and combat capabilities. One of the best mounts, the baby dragon, can be unlocked in the deluxe edition of the game and allows for free air dashes while gliding.

## 🛠️ Crafting and Gear Farming

### 🛠️ Crafting Gear

In DragonSword: Awakening, each character has unique gear that can be crafted to enhance their abilities. To craft gear, you will need to collect materials and select the main stat you want to prioritize. It's important to choose the main stat carefully, as it will be the strongest on your character's gear.

### 🌾 Farming Materials

Farming materials in DragonSword: Awakening can be done through various methods, including completing missions and exploring the world. It's essential to manage your resources efficiently and prioritize farming materials that are most needed for your characters.
