## Character Builds

### Lute
Lute is the protagonist and central hero of the game. Known for her versatile abilities, she can adapt to various combat situations.

**Builds**:
- **Swordmaster**: Focus on high damage and reliable combos.
- **Archer**: Emphasize long-range attacks and status effects.

### Mercenary Companions
Mercenary companions are unique allies that can be recruited during the game. Each companion has their own combat style and abilities.

**Companions**:
- **Thief**: Excels in stealth and critical hits.
- **Healer**: Specializes in support and healing abilities.

## Tag-Team Combat

### Understanding the System
Tag-team combat is central to DragonSword: Awakening. Switching between heroes allows you to create continuous attacks and activate strategic combinations.

### Tips for Success
- Learn the abilities and strengths of each hero.
- Time your character swaps to exploit enemy weaknesses.
- Use status effects to enhance your attacks.

## FAQ

**What is the game's main story about?**
DragonSword: Awakening follows the journey of Lute and her companions as they explore the Continent of Orbis and awaken the legendary Dragon Sword.

**How many heroes are available in the game?**
The game features 19 playable heroes and companions that can be unlocked through gameplay progression.

**What is the tag-team system?**
The tag-team system allows you to switch between heroes during combat, creating continuous attacks and strategic combinations.

**Is the game multiplayer?**
DragonSword: Awakening is a single-player game, but it features a rich storyline and diverse characters to explore.
