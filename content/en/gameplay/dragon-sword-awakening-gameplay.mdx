### 🏅 Building the Ideal Team

One of the most critical aspects of DragonSword: Awakening is building the perfect team. Each character has unique abilities and playstyles, so it's essential to choose characters that complement each other. Here are some top picks for your team:

- **Charlotte**: A versatile ninja with high mobility and damage output.
- **Johnny**: A classic DMC character with a range of abilities and a unique air dash.

### 🎯 Character Progression

Character progression is key to success in DragonSword: Awakening. As you level up, you'll unlock new abilities and skills for your characters. Here are some tips to help you progress efficiently:

- **Complete the main storyline**: Unlock important progression systems and story-driven content.
- **Complete hero quests**: Exclusive quests for specific heroes that reward you with unique karma and exclusive karma points.
- **Dismantle karma**: If you have karma you don't want, you can dismantle it to get currency and an exclusive karma point.

### 🛡️ Gear and Crafting

Optimizing your gear and crafting the right items is crucial for success in combat. Here's how to get started:

- **Craft gear**: Go to the blacksmith and craft gear sets for your characters based on their elements and stats.
- **Reforge gear**: Reforge your gear to get the exact stats you want.
- **Farming materials**: Farm materials by completing missions, clearing dungeons, and exploring the world.

### 🌊 Underwater Exploration

DragonSword: Awakening offers a unique underwater exploration experience. Here's what you need to know:

### 🛣️ Combat System

The combat system in DragonSword: Awakening is complex but rewarding. Here are some tips to help you master it:

- **Cooldowns**: Hitting enemies reduces the cooldown on your skills.
- **Teamplay**: Use your team's synergy to deal maximum damage and take down enemies.

### 📜 FAQ

**What happens if I regret my class choice?**
Once you select a class in DragonSword: Awakening, the decision is permanent. However, you can create a new character if you want to explore a different class.

**Can I play as a Mage but use a sword?**
Yes, but it won't be as effective as choosing a class that complements that playstyle, like Knight or Rogue.

**Which class is best for solo play?**
Mage is ideal for solo play due to its powerful spells and ranged attacks. However, Rogue also works well if you want more mobility and hybrid play.
