### What is DragonSword: Awakening?

DragonSword: Awakening is a fantasy RPG that combines exploration, combat, and character progression. The game features a vast world to explore, a variety of characters to unlock, and a deep combat system that rewards players for mastering their skills.

## Character Progression and Unlocking

One of the core aspects of DragonSword: Awakening is character progression and unlocking. As you progress through the story, you'll unlock new characters to add to your team. Each character has unique abilities and playstyles, allowing you to tailor your team to your preferences.

### Unlocking Characters

To unlock new characters, you need to clear the story chapters. Each chapter rewards you with tickets, which you can use to unlock new characters from the exchange shop. Additionally, you can acquire more tickets by completing the hero quest line, which features exclusive quests for specific heroes.

### Character Builds

When building your team, consider the synergies between characters. Some characters work well together, while others may not be as effective in a team. Experiment with different builds to find the combination that suits your playstyle.

## Mounts and Exploration

Mounts play a crucial role in DragonSword: Awakening, as they allow you to explore the vast world more efficiently. Unlocking new mounts and mastering their skills will make exploration much easier.

### Unlocking Mounts

Mounts can be unlocked through mercenary leveling and story progression. Some mounts are tied to specific chapters in the story, while others can be unlocked through various activities and quests.

### Mount Skills

Each mount has unique skills that can enhance your exploration and combat abilities. Unlocking multiple variations of the same mount allows you to unlock additional skills that can be used on all mounts except a few unique ones.

## Combat System

The combat system in DragonSword: Awakening is complex yet rewarding. Mastering the combat system will allow you to progress through the game more efficiently.

### Basic Combat Mechanics

The basic combat mechanics include basic attacks, jump attacks, and dodge attacks. Each character has their own unique moveset, which can be enhanced through skill trees and awakening abilities.

### Skill Cooldowns

Skills in DragonSword: Awakening have cooldowns, which can be reduced by hitting enemies. This encourages players to focus on combo-building and skill usage to maximize their damage output.

### Mastering Combat

To master the combat system, it's important to understand the mechanics and how to use them effectively. Experiment with different strategies and team compositions to find what works best for you.

## Gear and Crafting

Gear plays a crucial role in enhancing your characters' abilities. Crafting gear allows you to customize your characters' builds and increase their effectiveness in combat.

### Crafting Gear

To craft gear, you need to collect materials from various sources, such as enemies, quests, and exploration. Once you have the materials, you can visit the blacksmith to craft gear for your characters.

### Gear Stats

Gear has various stats that affect your characters' abilities. Some stats are more important than others, so it's important to choose the right gear for your characters' playstyles.

## Farming and Resources

Farming and resource management are essential for progressing through DragonSword: Awakening. Efficiently farming materials and resources will help you craft gear and unlock new abilities.

### Farming Locations

There are several locations where you can farm materials and resources. Some are early game locations, while others are endgame content that require higher levels and gear to access.

### Efficient Farming

To efficiently farm, it's important to know the best locations to farm and how to optimize your farming routine. Experiment with different strategies to find what works best for you.
