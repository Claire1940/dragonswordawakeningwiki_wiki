## Story Progression and Character Unlocking

DragonSword: Awakening's story mode is a great way to unlock new characters and understand the game's lore. By completing each chapter of the story, you'll be rewarded with tickets that can be exchanged for characters in the exchange shop. Additionally, completing hero quests offers exclusive karma for specific heroes, which can be used to unlock additional skills and abilities.

### Unlocking Characters

- **Story Mode**: Complete each chapter to earn tickets and unlock characters.
- **Hero Quests**: Exclusive quests for specific heroes, rewarding exclusive karma.
- **Dismantling Karma**: Exchange unwanted karma for currency and other exclusive karma.

## Mount System

The mount system in DragonSword: Awakening adds a new layer of exploration and character customization. Each mount has unique skills that can be upgraded by unlocking additional variations. The deluxe edition includes a baby dragon, allowing for air dashes and gliding.

### Mounts

- **Skills**: Unlock additional skills with each mount variation.
