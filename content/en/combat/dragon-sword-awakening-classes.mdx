## 🏅 Top Tier Classes

### 1. Charlotte
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Stealth, Poison Dart
- **Why it's top-tier**: Charlotte's ability to apply multiple status effects and her fluid skill combinations make her a formidable force on the battlefield. Her stealth skill allows her to bypass enemies and set up critical hits, while her poison dart can deal significant damage over time.

### 2. Roxie
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Scarlet Spiral, Blood Rain
- **Why it's top-tier**: Roxie is a high-mobility DPS with excellent range and crowd control abilities. Her Scarlet Spiral is a powerful kick that can chain multiple enemies, and her Blood Rain is a rapid combo that applies bleeding effects.

### 3. Tracya
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Sanctification, Celestial Judgment
- **Why it's top-tier**: Tracya's chain attack range and stunning effects make her a great support character. Her Sanctification skill can extend her normal attacks, while her Celestial Judgment can create a shockwave that stuns enemies.

### 4. Donna
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Chocolate, Chocolate Shield
- **Why it's top-tier**: Donna's unique ability to summon a colossal ice golem, Chocolate, as an extra warrior provides powerful crowd control. Her skills allow her to control the golem's actions and absorb damage.

## 🏆 Mid Tier Classes

### 5. Alex
- **Role**: Tank/Support
- **Skills**: Drop, Defense Pose
- **Why it's mid-tier**: Alex is a tank that focuses on defense and crowd control. His Drop skill is a multi-strike attack that can push enemies back, and his Defense Pose can block attacks and initiate counterattacks.

### 6. Erin
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Charge Spear, Hero
- **Why it's mid-tier**: Erin is a great option for those who enjoy direct combat. Her Charge Spear attack can launch enemies into the air, and her Hero skill creates a flag that boosts attack power for her and nearby allies.

### 7. Carrin
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Fox March, Fox Chase
- **Why it's mid-tier**: Carrin is a reliable spellcaster who specializes in burning effects and stunning. Her Fox March summons a group of fire foxes that explode on impact, while her Fox Chase can summon a larger fox that also applies burning effects.

### 8. Rena
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Rising Dragon, Windslash
- **Why it's mid-tier**: Rena is a great choice for players looking to add a powerful aerial attacker to their team. Her Rising Dragon and Windslash skills can both launch enemies into the air, making her a great choice for combo attacks.

## 🎮 Low Tier Classes

### 9. Scion
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Sacred Impact, Sacred Smash
- **Why it's low-tier**: Scion is a decent spellcaster who can provide some team bonuses, but his slow-moving attack skills can be a drawback.

### 10. Tart
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Lightning Impact, Lightning Bubble
- **Why it's low-tier**: Tart is a decent electric damage dealer with a minor healing ability, but her playstyle is not as engaging as other characters.

### 11. Astrid
- **Role**: DPS/Support
- **Skills**: Siege Assault, Area Shot
- **Why it's low-tier**: Astrid is a ranged archer with no ability to apply status effects. Her playstyle is more passive and can be vulnerable to enemy crowds.
