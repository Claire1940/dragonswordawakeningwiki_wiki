## 🏛️ The World of Orbis

DragonSword: Awakening is set in the beautiful and mysterious world of Orbis. As you explore, you'll encounter various environments, from lush forests to ancient ruins, each with its own unique challenges and rewards. The world is vast and full of secrets waiting to be discovered, so be sure to keep your eyes open and your senses sharp.

### 🌿 Forests and Jungles

The forests and jungles of Orbis are home to a variety of creatures and hidden treasures. These areas are perfect for training your heroes and gathering resources. Be on the lookout for rare herbs and materials that can be used to upgrade your gear and abilities.

### 🏰 Ancient Ruins

Ancient ruins scattered across Orbis are filled with puzzles, traps, and powerful artifacts. These locations are often guarded by powerful enemies, so be prepared for a challenging encounter. If you can overcome the obstacles, you'll be rewarded with valuable loot and new knowledge.

### 🌊 Water Bodies

Water bodies in Orbis are often the site of important events and encounters. These areas can be dangerous, but they also offer unique opportunities for exploration and discovery. Be sure to check your equipment and skills before diving into the depths.

## 🌟 Characters and Combat

DragonSword: Awakening features a diverse cast of characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles. You'll be able to build a team of heroes that suits your preferences and playstyle. Here's a breakdown of the different character types:

### 🤵 Hero

Heroes are the main characters in DragonSword: Awakening. They have unique abilities and playstyles that can change the tide of battle. As you progress, you'll unlock new heroes to add to your team.

### 🛡️ Companions

Companions are allies that join your party and provide additional support and combat options. They can be equipped with weapons and items to enhance their abilities.

### 🎯 Tag-Team Combat

Tag-team combat is a key mechanic in DragonSword: Awakening. By switching between heroes, you can create powerful combinations and exploit enemy weaknesses. Mastering this system is crucial for success in battle.

Building a strong team is essential for success in DragonSword: Awakening. Here are some tips to help you create the perfect team:
