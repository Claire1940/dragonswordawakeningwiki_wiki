## 🏹 Combat Mechanics
### Tag-Team Switching
Switch between heroes during combat to create continuous attacks and activate strategic combinations. Timing character swaps around enemy openings is essential.

### Status Ailments
Use different abilities to apply status effects and create opportunities for stronger follow-up attacks. Status-based strategies are central to team synergy.

### Combos & Chains
Combine normal attacks, skills, and character changes to create longer attack sequences. Effective combos increase damage output and combat control.

### Dodging & Parrying
Avoid enemy attacks with precise defensive timing and create openings for counterattacks. Defensive awareness is important against powerful enemies.

### Elemental Weaknesses
Use appropriate abilities and character choices to exploit enemy weaknesses. Adjusting your team for different opponents improves battle efficiency.

### Ultimate Skills
Powerful abilities provide burst damage and major combat advantages. Saving ultimate skills for important moments can turn difficult battles around.
